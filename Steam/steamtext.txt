[h3]Description[/h3]
A code mod that handles undoing certain actions when playing Airport CEO. Press f1 in game to change the keybinding for undoing.

Current support undo action:
- Placing items (chairs, runway foundations and way more)
- Placing floor variants (wood to marble)
- Placing walls and foundations
- Hiring employees
- Firing employees

[h3]Features[/h3]
- Add option to undo certain actions
